Diffusion is the movement of molecules from an area of ____ concentration to an ____ concentration
A
High, low
B
Low, high
C
Low, low,
D
High, high
Explanation: 

Detailed explanation-1: -Final answer: Movement of molecules from their higher concentration to their lower concentration is called diffusion.

Detailed explanation-2: -In diffusion, particles move from an area of higher concentration to one of lower concentration until equilibrium is reached. In osmosis, a semipermeable membrane is present, so only the solvent molecules are free to move to equalize concentration.

Detailed explanation-3: -Osmosis is a passive transport process during which water moves from areas where solutes are less concentrated to areas where they are more concentrated.
